Welcome to IDEAR

This document contains course notes for a class I’m expecting to never be taught. Much of this course outline draws from Hadley Wickham’s 2012 stat405 class, as well as his wonderful textbooks R for Data Science (with Garrett Grolemund) and Advanced R. I’ve updated some elements, distilled others, and not kept much of the content, but the ideas and steering come very directly from those works.

The class is budgeted as a thirteen week course composed of thirty-nine lectures. There are five assignments and zero exams.
